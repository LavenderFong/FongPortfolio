Macaron The Crow

Feburary 2025

"I wanted to create a cute little animation that just had an animal during animal things, so I chose a crow. Crows are one of my favorite animals because they are very intelligent, and they love to collect trinkets. This is the first animation where all the special effects I created in my room. The ruffling sound was me moving my hands in a fake flower, the CLOP sound, from the beak, is just me clapping, then the flapping sound is from a dress I own. The background ambience noises are from two separate free sound clips I found online. "
